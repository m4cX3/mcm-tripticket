Use of vehicles for Official Trips

1. MMCM ensures the use of vehicles including but not limited to MMCM official vehicles, Officer’s Car Plan Vehicles, and personal vehicles to support business activities and in the performance of employee’s regular duties and functions.

2. MMCM ensures that company drivers or any authorized employee know and abide to the driving laws and regulations in all areas when using the vehicle in official business.

3. MMCM establishes mechanism to ensure that company vehicles are in accordance with all applicable laws and regulations and are being subjected to periodic maintenance.

4. Prioritization on the use of vehicles is based on the nature, purpose, and urgency of task.

5. Reimbursement of gasoline and toll expenses is subject to the terms and conditions prescribe by institution.
